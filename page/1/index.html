A message from the team

Dear KJ Rafflesians,

AY 2021-2022, has truly been an unforgettable year for the student council. Yes, most of the activities we held were presented to you through your computer screens. However, we truly hope that you had fun participating in all of them, as much as we did preparing them for you.

Your support and participation were the driving forces behind our efforts to put activities together, knowing that someone would enjoy them.

This photobooth has been the final activity prepared by the student council of academic year 2021-2022, and by having it on-site, we really hoped that you would have much fun with your friends and teachers. We hope your photos came out as you expected, if not better. 

Thank you for everything, Rafflesians! See you all next year. 

~ RCS KJ AY 2021-2022 Student Council
